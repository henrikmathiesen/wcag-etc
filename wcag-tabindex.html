Give them tabindex=0 to make them tabable in the normal document flow. Tab order can be changed by providing other numbers.

Can also give an element tabindex="-1", then it will be removed from the natural taborder but stil focusable with JavaScript.

Dont play around with custom tabindex (tabindex=2,3 etc), it can become a maintenance nightmare. The only tabindex values we should use are -1 and 0
